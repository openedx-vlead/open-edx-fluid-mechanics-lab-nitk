Objective:

To find the force exerted by the jet on stationary plate.

Steps:

    1. When you click Impact of jet simulation, a window will open like shown below.

    1. Now click on “Select Button” to select different plate conditions.

    1. Click and drag the “Triangular Button” to set the diameter of the jet.

    1. Now click and drag the “Triangular Button” to set the velocity of jet.

    1. Finally the force exerted by the jet on the stationary plate for the given diameter and velocity will be obtained.
