Objective:

To determine the coefficient of discharge of Rectangular notch.

Steps:

    1. Open the Calibration of Rectangularnotch experiment and click on the next button shown at the bottom right corner.

    1. Start the pump by clicking on green button and then click on the next button.

    1. Click on the inlet valve to rotate it.

    1. Click on hand to rotate the inlet valve and click on the next button.

    1. Note the initial reading of the hook gauge and click on the hook gauge.

    1. Note the head of water, theoretical discharge of rectangular notch and click the next button.

    1. Note the initial reading of the hook gauge and click on the hook gauge.

    1. Note the head of water, actual discharge of V-notch and click the next button.

    1. Note the coefficient of discharge for rectangular notch.

    1. Repeat the same procedure, after certain trials, click on the red button to stop the pump.
